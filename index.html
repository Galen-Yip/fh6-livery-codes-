How to Apply These Livery Codes in FH5 (and Carry Over to FH6)

  1. Open Forza Horizon 5 and enter the Garage (FH6 will use the same flow at launch).
  2. Select your car and tap Customize → Livery Editor → Search Designs.
  3. Enter the design name or Creator ID exactly as shown on the card above.
  4. Pick the design from the result list, hit Download and apply it to your vehicle.
  5. Once Forza Horizon 6 launches, your downloaded liveries automatically transfer via your Personal Vault — no re-entering codes required.
