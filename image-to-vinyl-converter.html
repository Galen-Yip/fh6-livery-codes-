Step 2 · Convert Your Image to Vinyl Shapes

1Prepare a PNG or JPG image. Transparent PNG with a single subject works best (logos, anime portraits, flag patterns).

2Run forza-painter.exe and click Open Image.

3Choose a shape budget (e.g. 2000 shapes for a balanced result on most cars).

4Click Geometrize — the GPU-accelerated engine will trace your image into FH-compatible primitives in 30-90 seconds.

5Preview the result inside the tool. Reduce shapes if file feels heavy, increase for more detail.

Step 3 · Import the Decals into the Livery Editor

1Launch Forza Horizon 5 (or FH6 once it ships — same flow), enter the Garage and pick your car.

2Open Customize → Livery Editor, create a new vinyl group on the panel you want to paint.

3Alt-tab back to forza-painter and click Inject. The tool sends each shape to the editor exactly as if you were drawing them by hand — that's why it's anti-cheat safe.

4Wait until injection finishes (1-3 minutes for 2000 shapes). Save the vinyl group, then place and resize it on your car.

5Save the entire livery and (optionally) Share Design so the community can find it via your Creator ID — that's how the codes on our homepage are built.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is forza-painter safe for online multiplayer?

A: Yes. The tool only sends keyboard/mouse-equivalent input to draw shapes inside the official Livery Editor. It does not modify game files, memory or save data, so it cannot trigger anti-cheat or affect your online status.

Q: Will forza-painter work on FH6 at launch?

A: The community fork bvzrays/forza-painter-fh6 is already tracking the FH6 beta builds. The original repo is also expected to update within the first weeks after launch. Until then, run it on FH5 — designs you save now will carry over to FH6 via your Personal Vault.

Q: What image format gives the best decal quality?

A: Transparent PNG with high contrast and a single clear subject. Avoid heavy JPG compression artifacts — they create messy edge shapes. Aim for 1024-2048 px on the longest side.

Q: My injection stalls halfway through. What now?

A: Reduce the shape count to ~1500, make sure the FH window is in Borderless Windowed mode, and disable any overlay (Discord, GeForce Experience). Restart both apps and re-inject.

Q: Where can I share my finished livery?

A: Use the in-game Share Design flow. Once it's public, your Creator ID + design name can be searched by anyone — exactly the same format we use on our livery codes library.
